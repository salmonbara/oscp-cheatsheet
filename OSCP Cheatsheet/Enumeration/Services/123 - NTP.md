# NTP

`123/udp open ntp`

## Commands

#NTP #UDP #Enumeration
Synchronize local clock with target.

```sh
sudo ntpdate <TARGET_IP>
```

#NTP #UDP #Enumeration
Enumerate NTP with Nmap scripts.

```sh
nmap -sU -sV --script "ntp* and (discovery or vuln) and not (dos or brute)" -p 123 <TARGET_IP>
```
